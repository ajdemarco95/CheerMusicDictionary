#Tracking Order Guidelines

Order Guidelines

  • Keep each notes section between 2-8 bars at a time. Please use the format "Bar 6 / Beat 5” referring to the 5th 8 count of 8 count number 6 of the mix (or referred to as measure 6 / count 5)


  • Try to use the genre or synth definitions from this site whenever possible, and when not using those, use linked YouTube references to best describe what you’re looking for. Make sure to include the exact timestamp of the video (i.e. 2:31) and best description of the sound / what you like about it.


Rush Delivery:

If the order deadline is within 24 hours of order submission, a 20% rush delivery fee will be added to the order total.


Revisions:

All paid orders come with revisions, as long as the revisions are submitted within 7 days of order delivery during the September through November busy season, and 14 Days of Delivery between the December and August off season. After the revision time frame, that order is considered completed and is no longer eligible for revisions.
